About

Within the US, the Electoral College is the process that determines the presidency every four years. An archaic and undemocratic system that has contradicted the majority opinion five times in history, the process would best be replaced by a popular vote.

Learn More

History

The goal of the Electoral College was not to be democratic. Instead, it was the product of political compromises that dates back all the way to the Constitutional Convention, and whose problems still linger today.
